Datadog is a cloud application monitoring and analytics tool. You can use it to compute performance metrics and event monitoring for infrastructure and cloud services. In addition, Datadog can monitor services such as servers, databases and tools. With TestMu AI and Datadog integration, you can log bugs while perform [cross browser testing](https://www.lambdatest.com/) of your websites (and web apps) from the TestMu AI platform to Datadog.

## One-Click Bug Logging With Datadog[â](https://www.testmuai.com/support/docs/datadog-integration#one-click-bug-logging-with-datadog "Direct link to One-Click Bug Logging With Datadog")

* * *

  1. Select **Real Time Testing** from the left menu.

  2. Enter the test URL, select browser, browser versions, OS and resolution and press **START**.

![Image](https://www.testmuai.com/support/assets/images/test-environments-e95194785cce8aa2c4db9d9b5271ab67.webp)

  3. A cloud-based virtual machine will be launched, allowing you to conduct real-time testing of websites. After you've identified the issues, click on **Mark as Bug** icon in the left menu to capture a screenshot.

![Image](https://www.testmuai.com/support/assets/images/mark-as-bug-f5f494593b2da09ce1ebdd87e07fbc56.webp)

  4. After you've grabbed the screenshot, use the in-built image editor tools to mark any issues or tasks you've observed. After annotating the bugs, click on **Mark As Bug**.

![Image](https://www.testmuai.com/support/assets/images/mab-008952b978d0cb2732e04211ef53751a.webp)

  5. A Datadog ticket will open up. Fill the details like Service, Team, Assignee, Severity, State, Summary and Description. Then click on **Create Issue** and your bugs will be marked right away.

![Image](https://www.testmuai.com/support/assets/images/create-issue-c34c6540047114744184e82e081609de.webp)

  6. To view your marked bugs, visit **Issue Tracker** from the TestMu AI left navigation menu. You'll notice the marked bugs along with the Test environment details.

![Image](https://www.testmuai.com/support/assets/images/issue-tracker-ad4d1a4aaa1fcc160e8cee36ba399dc5.webp)

  7. Click on the right diagonal arrow icon to view the logged issues in Datadog.

![Image](https://www.testmuai.com/support/assets/images/diagarrow-dff5b0d1f705f721c24a0eb36d0f4f6f.webp)

You'll be taken to the Datadog interface where you can find your marked issues right away.

![Image](https://www.testmuai.com/support/assets/images/datadog-bugs-124ff80e00521d0348dcea467742b876.webp)
